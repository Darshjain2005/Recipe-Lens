About Recipe Lens

Bridging the gap between "What do I have?" and "What should I cook?"

Our Mission

"To make cooking hands-free, hygienic, and intelligent."

The Problem

Home cooks face two major struggles: Decision Paralysis and the "Messy Hand" Struggle (scrolling phones with dough-covered fingers).

The Solution

Recipe Lens is your AI Sous Chef. It recognizes raw ingredients from a photo and provides audio-guided recipes. Cook without touching your screen.

Key Technologies

Vision

Detects 15-20 raw ingredients instantly.

Voice

Hands-free control for hygienic cooking.

Intelligence

Smart database matching for Indian dishes.

Social Impact: SDG 12

Responsible Consumption & Production

We help reduce food waste by suggesting recipes based on ingredients you already have.

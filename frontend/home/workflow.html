How Recipe Lens Works

1

Smart Visual Scan

Simply point your camera at the ingredients on your table. Our AI instantly recognizes vegetables like tomatoes, onions, and capsicums in real-time.

2

Hands-Free Voice Input

Busy hands? Just speak! List your ingredients out loud, and the system listens. Say "DONE" to start the recipe search automatically.

3

Recipe Selection

The app suggests 3 perfect Indian dishes based on what you have. Simply say "Option 1" or "Option 2" to confirm your choice without touching the screen.

4

Interactive Chef Mode

Start cooking with audio guidance! The app reads steps one by one. Say "Next" to move forward or "Repeat" if you missed something.
